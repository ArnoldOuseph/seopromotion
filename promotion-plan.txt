Client: GreenPasture Cattle Farm

What product/service is being promoted?
GreenPasture Cattle Farm promotes ethically raised, grass-fed cattle and farm-fresh products. The focus is on sustainable farming practices, animal welfare, and providing high-quality natural products to local customers.

Who is the target audience?
Primary audience: Health-conscious individuals and families who prefer locally sourced, ethically raised, and minimally processed food.
Secondary audience: Local restaurants, cafés, and small retailers interested in premium farm-to-table suppliers.

Which platforms are most suitable (Instagram, YouTube, etc.)?
Instagram and Facebook: Best for farm photos, short videos, stories, weekly updates, and customer reviews.
YouTube: Best for longer behind-the-scenes videos, educational content on ethical farming, and transparency about farm practices.

Proposed Hashtags:
#GreenPastureFarm
#GrassFedCattle
#EthicalFarming
#SustainableAgriculture
#FarmFreshFood

Promotional Strategy (2–3 sentences):
GreenPasture Cattle Farm will build trust through consistent social media posting that highlights daily farm life, animal-first care, and sustainable practices. Instagram and Facebook will be used for engaging visuals, short clips, and announcements, while YouTube will support longer educational videos that improve credibility and reach. Targeted hashtags and local community engagement will help attract customers who value ethical and locally sourced food.

Note:
"GreenPasture Cattle Farm" is a fictional business created for the purpose of this academic assignment.
All branding, promotional strategies, and digital content were developed solely for learning and demonstration purposes.
